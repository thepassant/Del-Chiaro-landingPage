about us

Born in 1660 (other sources claim 1650, or 1680), the italian Anton-Maria Del Chiaro, a Florentine by birth, ... was the secretary of the Wallachian Prince Constantin Brâncoveanu as well as the tutor of the children of Ștefan Cantacuzino, another Wallachian prince. He published on 22 August 1718 in Venice the book titled “The History of the Modern Revolutions of Wallachia”
